About The Games

Our History

The Senior Games were founded in 2010 to combat ageism in sports and promote competitive longevity. Since then, we have grown from a local tournament to an internationally recognized event, hosting thousands of athletes annually. We celebrate the strength, agility, and enduring spirit of senior athletes from around the globe.
